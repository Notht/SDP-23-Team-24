About Good L.O.O.P.S.

Good L.O.O.P.S. stands for LOOPS Open Orchestrator Production System. Our mission is to provide a complete and open-source solution for industrial automation and control systems. We believe that our technology can make a difference in people's lives by improving productivity, efficiency, and safety.

Our system consists of a hardware platform, a software platform, and a set of tools and services that enable our users to easily design, deploy, and manage their automation and control systems.

Our Goals

Our goals are:

  • To make automation and control systems more accessible and affordable for everyone.
  • To promote the use of open-source technologies in the industrial sector.
  • To improve the quality of life for workers and communities by creating safer and more efficient working environments.
